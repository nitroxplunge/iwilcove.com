Computer Science student at the University of Colorado Boulder. Roboticist, programmer, skiier, nonprofit director.

What I'm Doing

I'm hard at work building robots for the 2023 season of the RoboMaster collegiate robotics competition while I continue my education as a Junior at CU Boulder. In my role as the President of the CU Robotics Club, my contributions range from robot control systems to business administration. We're a formally incorporated 501(c)(3) nonprofit charity, and are running to be the RoboMaster North America (RMNA) tournament hosts in 2024.

I'm passionate for future technologies and product thinking. I believe that many world-changing breakthroughs are technically achievable yet undervalued and thus not developed.
